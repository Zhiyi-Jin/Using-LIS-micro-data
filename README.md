### An exploration of income inequality by the use of LIS database
            
            
**Database**

LIS is a Luxembourg-based data center, which collects cross-national harmonized databases that allow international comparative research on income, wealth and employment outcomes using micro-data. It mainly targets at high-income and middle-income countries, covering mostly in Europe and North America, rapidly expanding to other continents from the late 1960s. Household composition, household consumption data, detailed breakdown of household and individual income data are included. More info: https://www.lisdatacenter.org/our-data/lis-database/
